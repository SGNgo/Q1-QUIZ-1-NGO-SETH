Toy Story

Toy Story is a movie where in toys come to life when humans aren't playing with them. It is one of the earlier Pixar films released.The plot revolves around a two toys: Woody and Buzz Lightyear. They go through many adventures together and aren't the best of friends at first.After all they went through they were able to figure things out and be there as friends for their owner: Andy. The film is followed by three sequels and is a well-known franchise to this day.
